Brand Identity Standard: Axion
1. Design Philosophy
The Axion logo is designed to communicate momentum, precision, and connectivity.
The Icon: A stylized, fluid "A" that doubles as a path or circuit. The integrated upward-pointing arrow represents scaling, growth, and the "forward-thinking" nature of the brand.
The Typography: A heavy, geometric sans-serif font provides a grounded, stable base to balance the dynamic energy of the icon.

3. Typography Guide
Primary Brand Typeface: Montserrat Bold or Inter ExtraBold.
Usage: Company name (AXION) only.
Secondary/Tagline Typeface: Montserrat Medium.
Usage: "Innovation • Technology • Solutions".
Styling: All Caps with high tracking (+200) to create a "premium" feel.
4. Visual Proportions & Scaling
To maintain the integrity of the design, observe the following:
The "X" Alignment: The tagline is horizontally centered under the "XION" portion of the name, providing a visual anchor.
Safe Zone: Maintain a "Clear Space" around the logo equal to the width of the letter "N". Do not place text or other graphics within this border.
Minimum Legibility: * Web: 180px wide (Full Logo).
Icon Only: 32px x 32px (Favicon/App Icon).
5. Logo Variations
Full Logo: The preferred version (Icon + Text) on a white or light grey background.
Reversed: White text and icon on a "Tech Blue" background for dark mode or merchandise.
Monochrome: Solid black or solid white for high-contrast documents or official stamps.
6. Usage Restrictions
Do Not rotate the icon or the arrow.
Do Not change the color of the arrow independently of the icon.
Do Not stretch or distort the proportions of the font.
